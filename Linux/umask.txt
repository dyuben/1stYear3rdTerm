find - command used in searching files

ex.
	find name_of_the_file

using find in wildcards:

* - all 
ex. find w*

? - no. of letter 
ex. find w???

t[a-z] - ta, tb, tc, ....
ex. find w[a-z]*
difference is you can specify what you want to find.
find w[a-d]

! - not
ex. find w[!a-z]*

^ - not
ex. find w[^a-z]*

umask - reverse the value of permissions, also gives the default value.

ex. umask 111
